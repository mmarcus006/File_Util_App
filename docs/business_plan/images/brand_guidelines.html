FDDSearch Brand Style Guide

Brand Overview

Mission statement:
FDDSearch.com is the definitive source for franchise disclosure documents, providing comprehensive access to thousands of franchise opportunities. We empower potential franchisees with the data they need to make informed investment decisions.

Brand personality: Authoritative, transparent, data‑driven — yet approachable and modern.

Voice & tone: Clear and concise. Jargon‑free explanations backed by facts. Encouraging action with confidence.

Logo Usage

Do

  • Maintain clear space at least the height of the orange circle around the mark.
  • Use primary navy #0B2D45 for document & wordmark on light backgrounds.
  • Use a white logo reversed out of #0B2D45 background for dark contexts.

Don't

  • Change colors of the mark elements.
  • Squash, stretch, rotate, or apply drop shadows.
  • Place over busy photography without sufficient contrast.

Minimum size: 64 px wide in digital contexts to preserve legibility.

Typography

Style Font Usage
Headlines (H1‑H3) system-ui / Inter, 600‑700 weight Page & card titles; keeps strong hierarchy in data‑heavy layouts.
Body copy system-ui / Inter, 400‑500 Paragraph text, table cells.
Code & numbers monospace, 500 Data points, HEX codes, schema snippets.

Use sentence‑case headings and keep line‑length ≤ 70 ch for optimal readability.

Iconography & Illustration

Use simple, 2‑stroke line icons set at 1.5 px. Primary color should be #15466A. Use #F57C00 only for actionable icons (download, alert).

Photography & Imagery

  • Prefer clean, well‑lit imagery of real franchise storefronts or entrepreneurs.
  • Avoid stock photos that feel overly staged or clichéd.
  • Apply a 10 % navy overlay (#0B2D45) on hero images for legible headline contrast.

Accessibility Checklist

  • All text/background combos ≥ WCAG AA contrast.
  • Focus indicators use #F57C00.
  • Buttons ≥ 44 px touch target.
  • ARIA labels for all interactive icons.
